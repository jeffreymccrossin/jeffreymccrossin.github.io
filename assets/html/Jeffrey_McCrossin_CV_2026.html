Highlights

  • Clinician-researcher specializing in family-centred and equity-oriented approaches to child and family mental health, neurodiversity, and caregiver support.
  • Over 15 years of clinical experience in family therapy, neurorehabilitation, and mental health across hospital and community settings.
  • Experience leading community-engaged research with Indigenous and diverse families.
  • Secured $840,995 in research funding and awards.
  • Author of 13 peer-reviewed publications on family well-being and caregiving.
  • Dedicated educator and clinical supervisor recognized for innovation in teaching and mentorship.
